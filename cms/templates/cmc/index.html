How It Works

The Medical Appointment Management System is designed to help clinics manage their daily operations efficiently. Here's how each part of the system works:

  • Appointments: Create and manage patient appointments, assigning doctors and times for each visit.
  • Patients: Keep a detailed record of each patient's personal information and medical history.
  • Billing: Generate invoices and track payment status for each appointment.
  • Reports: Generate insights and reports to improve clinic performance.

Guides

Follow these guides to get the most out of the system:

  • Creating an Appointment: Navigate to the 'Create Appointment' page, select a patient, doctor, and the time slot, then click 'Submit'.
  • Adding a New Patient: Go to 'Add Patient', fill out the personal and medical details, then save the record.
  • Managing Billing: After an appointment, go to the 'Billing' section, enter the cost details, and update the payment status.
